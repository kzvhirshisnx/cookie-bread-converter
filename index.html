🍪 Cookie Recipe

Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs, vanilla, then flour, soda, salt. Fold in chips. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10 min.

🍌 Banana Bread Recipe

Moist Banana Bread

  • 2 to 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups flour

Mix mashed bananas with butter, add other ingredients. Pour into loaf pan. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 50–60 min.
